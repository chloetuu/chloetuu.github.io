About Me

Hi! My name is Chloe Tu, and I'm currently a third-year student at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Finance. I spent my first year studying Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology but ultimately realized I wanted to use the analytical skills from my major as a tool within business. Because of that, I decided to switch my major and transfer schools so I could better pursue my interests in both data and business strategy.

At Northeastern, I'm involved in Delta Sigma Pi (DSP), a professional business fraternity. I currently hold the position of VP of Community Service this semester, which has been a really rewarding leadership experience. I am also part of a strategy consulting club called Huntington Strategy Group (HSG), where I work on a semester-long project with a real client. Through this experience, I collaborate with a team to help analyze problems and propose strategic solutions, which has been both challenging and exciting.

As for my professional experience, I previously worked at TJX as a Strategy and Analytics Co-op where I built dashboards using PowerBI, worked extensively with Excel, and analyzed large datasets to support business decisions. This upcoming June, I will be starting as a Treasury Co-op at Boston Consulting Group (BCG). I am really grateful for this opportunity and excited to continue growing my skills while learning more about financial operations and strategy in a professional setting.
