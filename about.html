Hobbies and Personal Interests

Outside of school and work, I enjoy spending time on hobbies that allow me to be creative, active, and explore new experiences.

One of my favorite things to do is cook and bake. I love trying new foods and experimenting with different recipes. My favorite foods are sushi and seafood boils, although seafood has recently become a bit of a sensitive topic after I got food poisoning from oysters. Even so, trying new restaurants and cuisines is something I always enjoy doing with friends and family.

Another big part of my life has always been soccer. I started playing when I was three years old and have loved the sport ever since. I primarily played as a striker and competed on both my high school team and a club team growing up. When I started college, I also played on a club team during my freshman year. Soccer has been a really important part of my life because it taught me discipline, teamwork, and how to stay motivated even in competitive environments.

I also enjoy more creative hobbies. Recently, I have been getting into thrifting and upcycling clothing. I have my own sewing machine, and I like finding pieces at thrift stores that I can modify or improve. It has become a fun creative outlet where I can experiment with different styles and learn new skills while giving old clothing a second life.

Traveling is another thing I really enjoy. This past summer I traveled to Bali with my family, and over winter break we visited Vietnam. These were actually my first times traveling outside of the United States, which made the experiences even more exciting. I loved being able to explore different cultures, try new foods, and see places that were completely different from home. More recently, I also traveled to Europe for spring break with some friends, where we visited Lisbon, Madrid, and Barcelona. It was really fun exploring those cities together and experiencing a new part of the world.

Some of My Favorite Things

  • Cooking and baking new recipes
  • Trying new restaurants and foods
  • Playing soccer
  • Thrifting and upcycling clothing
  • Traveling and exploring new cultures
